---
layout: recipe
title: Peanut Butter Banana Overnight Oats
image: peanut-butter-banana-overnight-oats.png
tags: Breakfast
category: Breakfast

preptime: 10 Minutes

ingredients:
- 1/2 Cup Rolled Oats
- 1 Banana, sliced
- 2/3 Cup Unsweetened Almond Milk
- 1/2 Tablespoon Chia Seeds
- 1 Tablespoon Protein Powder
- 1 Tablespoon Natural Creamy Peanut Butter
- 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
- 2 Teaspoons Maple Syrup
- 1 Pinch Salt

directions:
- Combine oats, chia seeds, maple, peanut butter, vanilla and salt and stir to combine. Add in milk and stir.
- Cover and refrigerate overnight or at least for about 4 hours. Add sliced bananas just before serving.
