title: Spicy Ranch Pretzels
image: spicy-ranch-pretzels.png
tags: Appetizers
category: Appetizers

ingredients:
- 1 Bag of Mini Pretzels, Twists
- 3/4 Cup of Olive or Avocado Oil
- 1 Hidden Valley Ranch Seasoning Packet
- 1 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
- 1 to 2 Tablespoons of Red Pepper Flakes

directions:
- Put all of the ingredients together in a gallon size bag.
- Shake the bag to coat all of the pretzels.
- Leave to soak 24 hours, flipping the bag every so often.
