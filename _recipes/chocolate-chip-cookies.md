---
layout: recipe
title: Chocolate Chip Cookies
image: chocolate-chip-cookies.png
tags: Dessert
category: Dessert

yield: About 4 Dozen Cookies

ingredients:
- 2 1/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
- 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
- 1 teaspoon Salt
- 1 Cup (2 sticks) unsalted Butter, softened
- 3/4 Cup Sugar
- 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar, packed
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla
- 2 Large Eggs
- 2 Cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

directions:
- Preheat oven to 375 degrees. 
- In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.
- In a large mixing bowl, beat butter, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla until creamy.
- Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
- Beat in flour mixture gradually.
- Stir in chocolate chips.
- Form balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet.
- Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown.
- Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes, and then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
