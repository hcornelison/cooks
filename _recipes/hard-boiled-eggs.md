---
layout: recipe
title: Hard Boiled Eggs
image: hard-boiled-eggs.png
tags: Breakfast
category: Breakfast

preptime: 1 Minute
cooktime: 13 Minutes

ingredients:
- 12 eggs (or as many as you want)

directions:
- Put the eggs in the bottom of a sauce pan.
- Fill the pan with cold water, 1 inch above the eggs.
- Bring to a rapid boil.
- Boil for 13 minutes.
- Remove from heat, and let sit for 30 minutes.
- Drain water, put cold water in the sauce pan over the eggs.
- Peel the eggs.
