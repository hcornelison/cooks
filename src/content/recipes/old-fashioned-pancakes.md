---
title: Old Fashioned Pancakes
image: old-fashioned-pancakes.png
tags: Breakfast
category: Breakfast

yield: 9 Pancakes
preptime: 15 Minutes
cooktime: 15 Minutes

ingredients:
- 2 1/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
- 2 Tablespoons Sugar
- 1 Tablespoon Baking Powder
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt
- 1/4 teaspoon Baking Soda
- 1 2/3 Cups Whole or Butter Milk
- 4 Tablespoons Butter, melted
- 2 Large Eggs
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla

directions:
- In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and baking soda until evenly mixed.
- Create a well in the center of the dry mixture and pour in the milk, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla.
- Gently whisk until just combined, it's okay if the batter is slightly lumpy.
- Preheat your griddle or nonstick skillet over medium heat (about 350°F) until a droplet of water sizzles on contact.
- Using a 1/2 cup measuring cup, pour the batter onto the hot griddle to form circles.
- Cook until small bubbles appear and the edges begin to set, about 2-3 minutes.
- Carefully flip the pancakes and cook until golden brown, another 1-2 minutes.
